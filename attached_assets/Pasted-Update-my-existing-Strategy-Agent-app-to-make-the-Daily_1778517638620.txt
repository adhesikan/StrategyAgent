2. DAILY AI OPPORTUNITY SCAN AS INVISIBLE ENGINE
====================================================

Create or update a backend/service layer called:

DailyOpportunityScanService

Purpose:
Run a daily scan across either:
- user watchlist
- connected brokerage positions
- selected universe
- fallback popular liquid symbols

It should generate simple candidate ideas for:
- Stock growth opportunities
- Bullish/bearish stock setups
- Options income ideas
- Defined-risk option trades
- Watchlist alerts
- Market/news/sentiment opportunities

Supported instruments:
- Stocks
- Long Calls
- Long Puts
- Covered Calls
- Cash-Secured Puts
- Debit Spreads
- Vertical Spreads

This service should reuse existing engines where possible:
- Opportunity Radar scoring
- Probability Engine
- News Sentiment Layer
- Instrument Selector
- Risk Preferences / Guardrails
- Broker data service
- Options chain service
- Strategy Scanner logic

Do not duplicate broker or scanner logic unnecessarily.

Service functions:
- getDailyIdeasForUser(userId)
- getGrowthIdeas(userId)
- getIncomeIdeas(userId)
- getStockIdeas(userId)
- getOptionIdeas(userId)
- getWatchlistAlerts(userId)
- getMarketSnapshot(userId)
- getBeginnerFriendlyIdeaCards(userId)

Return simple normalized objects:

DailyIdea {
  id
  userId
  symbol
  companyName
  category: growth | income | trade | market_alert
  instrumentType: stock | long_call | long_put | spread | covered_call | cash_secured_put
  title
  simpleSummary
  whyItAppeared
  riskLevel: low | medium | high
  grade
  score
  maxRisk
  capitalNeeded
  potentialReward
  timeHorizon
  sentimentLabel
  brokerConnected
  dataMode
  sourceEngine
  createdAt
}

Important:
For novice-facing cards, hide technical score breakdown by default.
Allow “View Details” to expand into the existing advanced details.

11. FINAL UX GOAL
====================================================

A novice trader should be able to:

1. Open the app
2. Understand what to do within 5 seconds
3. Choose Stocks, Options, Growth, or Income
4. Enter capital and max risk
5. See simple ideas immediately
6. Learn why an idea appeared
7. Paper trade or review safely
8. Only submit live order after explicit confirmation

The app should feel:
simple, helpful, modern, and intelligent

NOT:
overwhelming, technical, or cluttered.
